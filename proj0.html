PERUVIAN TOURING

Peru has so many places where people can visit. Due to this country has different regions, people
will be able to choose one of each region and it also depends of their climate , food , etc preferences.

Here is a list of a few peruvian regions:
  1. Costa.
  2. Sierra.
  3. Selva.
  4. Peruvian sea.

Costa region has a tropical climate and tourist tend to visit this region when it is hot because it is when most touring places are available.
Here is a list of the most popular Costa region places and the most demanding dishes:
  • Lima.
  • Piura.
LIMA
Lima is the capital of Peru where you can find all the different food of the three regions. The place
most visited in Lima is THe Plaza de Armas or The Plaza Mayor de Lima. The Plaza de Armas is one of the
most important place in Lima because is the place where Lima was Founded.
Lima climated is tropical and humid and it is constantly changing.
PIURA
Piura has the largest variety of marine species in Peru thast why the most popular dish in Piura
is made of fish.The Ceviche is one of the most demanding dishes by tourist and also Arroz Con Mariscos
and Parihuela which all of these were made of marine species.
The climate in Piura is often hot, that is why most people liked to visit it and all of it different beaches.

SIERRA
more info!

Sierra region is known for having a cold climate all the year.People visit it because is an interesting
region which has an enigmatic history about gods , empires and Incas.This region presents a lot of mountains
and remote civilizations.
The most popular places in Sierra region are:
  • Cusco.
  • Puno.
CUSCO
Also called Ciudad Inca where the first Peruvian civilizations emerged.
People will find archaeological remains of Inca cities. One of the most important
places in Cusco is Machu Picchu.Machu Picchu is a city hidden among the great mountains
of the region is a great place for touring.The Cusco gastronomy stands out for the use of
traditional carb-rich native foods such as potato, corn and quinoa. Also, for the different
types of chili peppers and varied types of meat such as cuy, alpaca, chicharrón, ram, and others.
PUNO
Puno has a similar history as Cusco.The Titicaca lake(One of the most atractive places in Puno) is part of
an important historical legend tells how two important kings emerged from the lake who were responsible for
giving rise to the Inca civilizationaion.

Selva region has an extreme climate because it is diferent in the day (sometimes hot or rainy) and in the night(often hot).
This region has a huge variety of animals and plants which also are use as food and as medicine. The most visited places in Selva region are:
  • Loreto.
  • Iquitos.
LORETO
It offers the grandiosity of the Amazon landscape; its river wealth (in its lands runs the Amazon River,
( the longest and most abundant in the world); the flora and fauna of extraordinary variety and its jungle tribes.
A dense tropical forest covers its entire territory, with gigantic and cheaters trees forming a sort of roof that
prevents the sun from entering its soil.
IQUITOS
One of the aspects most emphasized in Iquitos is the Main Church. This Catholic Church finds a Plaza de Armas
full of palm trees and its height exceeds to all the rest buildings of the city. The construction architect of the Tower Eiffel,
Gustave Eiffel, also designed the “House of Iron” that is in the rim of the same square and as its name indicates it it is constructed of metal.
